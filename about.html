ABOUT ME

A passionate healer & guide

I started off as a teacher before starting my journey towards healing people. It was a roller coaster ride which helped me see life from another perspective and how it poses a different challenge to different people which inturn helps them become a better version of themselves.

I’m a single mom who has an adventurous soul that is shared with my teenage son. I am a person who sees this world as one big family and tries to treat every stranger as a friend yet to be met. I have a different outlook on life which i attained through innumerous travels and interactions with people of different cultures and places with many untold stories.

My life hasn’t exactly been a happy fairytale someone tells as a bedtime story. But because of these tough challenges, risks and doubts, i was able to become the person i am today and gave me the ability to teach from real life experiences rather than a hypothesis. This hard earner versatility and ability to cope and adapt to varying scenarios and connect with my clients whom i consider part of my family, gives me another dimension of opportunities to dig deeper into the heart of a person and guide them into achieving peace with themselves. I believe this proven quality of mine helps my clients from all walks of life achieve breakthroughs no matter how difficult it may seem to be.
